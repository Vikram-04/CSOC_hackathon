About ReflectHub

ReflectHub is a public reflection platform designed for college events. Unlike private feedback forms, ReflectHub lets students, volunteers, and hosts share their stories and insights openly — helping others learn from different perspectives.

It’s a community reflection wall — not a suggestion box.
